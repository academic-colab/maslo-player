Getting Content Packs

To view and obtain new content packs, you will need a network connection. Both Wi-Fi and data networks (such as 3G) work. Once you are online tap on the "Shop" button. You will see a list of all available content packs including the ones already installed on your device. If you have already installed packs - or if your app comes with pre-installed packs - you will see an "Installed" button to the right of some of the packs on your device. These packs are available for viewing. If you want to re-install them you will need to delete them first.

Content packs not already installed will have an "Install" button on their right, and tapping that button will guide you through an installation dialogue. You will receive a message when the pack installation is completed and will be able to view the newly installed content pack right away.

In case a content pack you already installed has been updated since you last visited the store, the pack will display an "Update" button to the right. Like the "Install" button, tapping the "Update" button will guide you through the update process.

Viewing Content Packs

You can enter a content pack from either "Home" or "Shop" by tapping on the pack name. This will bring you to the pack's table of contents where you will see a list of all the sections in a pack. From the "Home" screen you can also sort your packs alphabetically. Tap on the "Sort" button in the upper left corner of the screen. Tap again to sort in descending order. Once inside a pack you will see a table of contents. tapping on one of the sections will open that section. To navigate forward or backward, you can either swipe left or right on your device or use the left/right buttons in the header. The "Back" button will bring you back to the content pack's table of contents.

In addition to text items, packs can contain a variety of media:

Images

You can tap on the displayed image to enlarge it. Tapping on the enlarged image again will close it.

Audio

On iOS audio content can be played back inline by tapping on the "play" button. During playback you can pause the audio at any time by tapping the "pause" button. You can also use the scroll bar to navigate within the audio file.

On Android audio content is opened in a new video player window. Leaving the video player or completing the content brings you back to the audio content start screen.

Video

Video content is opened in a new device-specific video player window. Leaving the video player or completing the video brings you back to the video content start screen.

Quizzes

Once you have opened the first question in the quiz, swipe will be deactivated. Although you can leave a quiz by using any of the navigation buttons, once you submit an answer for a quiz item, you cannot revise any earlier answers. Using the top bar navigation arrow buttons will bring you to the next/previous section of the current content pack.

Deleting Content Packs

To delete a content pack, you need to be on the "Home" screen. There you can see a list of all installed content packs. In the upper right corner of the screen is an "Edit" button. Tap the "Edit" button and red and white delete icons will show up next to each content pack. Tap on the icon next to the content pack you want to delete. This will cause a "Delete" button to appear to the right of the selected content pack name. Tap "Delete" and you will be guided through the pack deletion.

Search

Various search options are available depending on what screen you are in when you press the search button:
  • From the 'Home' screen, perform a search in all installed content packs
  • From the table of contents of a pack, perform a search within the selected content pack.
  • From the 'Shop' screen, perform a search in all available content packs. This requires a network connection.
  • From within a section of a pack, perform a text search of that page
Note that quizzes are not included in the search.

Settings

The settings menu allows you to pick a default behavior for quiz feedback:
  • feedback always on
  • feedback always off
  • have each quiz ask (the default)

Additionally, you can choose which server to retrieve content packs from.
